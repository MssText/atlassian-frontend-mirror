## Appearance

The most common variations used for lozenges.

### Default

A default status. Use for neutral, to-do or not started, unavailable and minor states.

<Example Component={DefaultLozenge} componentName="@atlaskit/lozenge"/>

### Success

Use success as a constructive status, such as available, completed, approved, resolved or added states.

<Example Component={SuccessLozenge} componentName="@atlaskit/lozenge"/>

### Removed

Use removed as a critical or problematic status such as errors, declined, deleted or failed states.

<Example Component={RemovedLozenge} componentName="@atlaskit/lozenge"/>

### In progress

Commonly used as an in-flight, in progress or accepted status.

<Example Component={InProgressLozenge} componentName="@atlaskit/lozenge"/>

### New

Use as a status for new features, newly created or help states.

<Example Component={NewLozenge} componentName="@atlaskit/lozenge"/>

### Moved

Use for items that have been changed and require attention, such as updated, modified or moved.

<Example Component={MovedLozenge} componentName="@atlaskit/lozenge"/>


## Max width

The default width length for the lozenge is 200 px. Anything longer than that will be truncated and replaced with ellipsis.
